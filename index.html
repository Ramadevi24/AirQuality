HEALTH RECOMMENDATIONS

Last refreshed: 1:00 PM (UTC +4)

When the air quality index (AQI) hits orange, red, purple, or maroon levels, it's essential to take proactive measures to safeguard your well-being. Follow these guidelines to minimize the impact of elevated air pollution levels on your health.

  • Mask Usage

    Mask Usage

    If outdoor exposure is unavoidable,consider wearing a mask. Note that not all masks provide equal protectionagainst particle pollution.

    Cloth or dust masks may fall short, but well-fittedN95 or KN95 masks offer better filtration capabilities and can be beneficial onhigh AQI days.
